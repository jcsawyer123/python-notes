Comparison Operators
Operator | Meaning
    < | Less Than
    <= | Less Than or Equal To
    > | Greater Than
    >= | Greater Than or Equal To
    == | Equal To
    != | Not Equal

Augmented Assignment Operators
Operator | Equivalent Statement | What
    += 1 | x = x + 1 | Add to
    -= 1 | x = x - 1 | Subract
    *= 1 | x = x * 1 | Multiply
    /= 1 | x = x / 1 | Divide
    ?- 1 | x = x % 1 | Modulus(?)
